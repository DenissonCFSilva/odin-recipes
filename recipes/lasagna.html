Lasagna

Italian delicacy

The lasagna is a widely known dish, served almos everywhere.

If you ever wanted to make your own, by yourself, but never had someone teach you how to do it, we're here to help. Here's how to do it.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 (8 ounce) package fresh mushrooms, coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 6 cups prepared marinara sauce
  • ¼ cup water, or as needed (Optional)

Pasta

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles

Ricotta Filling
